# Calibration: Business Viability Risk

Weak, adequate, and strong examples for scoring Business Viability Risk (B1-B5).

## Strong (Score 5)

- Unit economics model showing cost to build, cost to serve, and expected revenue
- Legal review sign-off for regulated domains (data privacy, financial, healthcare)
- Go-to-market alignment: sales can position it, marketing can message it, support can handle it
- Competitive moat analysis: how defensible is this advantage?
- Pricing strategy validated with customer willingness-to-pay data

**Example signal:** "Unit economics show $2.40 cost to serve per user/month against $12 ARPU; legal cleared data processing for EU markets; sales team trained on positioning document."

## Adequate (Score 3)

- Business case sketched but not validated with real financial data
- Legal awareness but no formal review completed
- General alignment with business strategy but GTM plan not detailed
- Competitive advantage assumed but not analyzed against specific competitors

**Example signal:** "We think this fits our enterprise strategy and should drive upsells. Finance hasn't reviewed the unit economics yet but the margins should be fine."

## Weak (Score 1-2)

- No analysis of unit economics, margins, or cost to serve
- Legal and compliance implications not evaluated
- Go-to-market plan absent — sales and marketing not consulted
- "We'll monetize later" mindset
- No competitive analysis

**Example signal:** "This is a great feature that users will love. We'll figure out the business model after launch."

## Worked Example

**Scenario:** Team proposes adding HIPAA-compliant data storage for healthcare customers.

| Criterion | Evidence Presented | Score |
|-----------|-------------------|-------|
| B1: Business Model Alignment | Enterprise tier expansion; targets $500K+ ARR segment | 5 — Clear strategic fit |
| B2: Legal Compliance | HIPAA gap analysis completed; BAA template drafted | 4 — In progress but not signed off |
| B3: Unit Economics | Infrastructure cost modeled at $8/user/month; premium tier priced at $45/user/month | 5 — Validated economics |
| B4: Operationalization | Sales team briefed; marketing has healthcare case studies planned | 4 — Planned but not yet executed |
| B5: Competitive Position | 2 of 3 competitors lack HIPAA compliance; first-mover in segment | 5 — Clear moat |

**Dimension Score:** 5/5 — Comprehensive viability assessment with minor gaps in execution.
