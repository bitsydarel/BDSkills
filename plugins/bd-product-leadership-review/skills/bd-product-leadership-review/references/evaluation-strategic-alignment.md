# Extended Element: Strategic Alignment (P6)

Does this initiative advance the company's current strategic priorities, or is it a detour?

### Evaluation Questions

1. Can the team articulate which company-level strategic priority this serves?
2. Is the connection between this initiative and the strategic priority direct (not three levels of indirection)?
3. If the company's strategy shifted tomorrow, would this initiative still be valuable?
4. Does this initiative compete for resources with higher-priority strategic goals?
5. Has leadership explicitly confirmed this initiative's strategic fit?

### Element Scoring

- **Present**: Clear, direct connection to a stated company priority; leadership confirms alignment; initiative would survive a strategy review
- **Partial**: Loosely connected to strategy; connection requires explanation; might be deprioritized in a strategy review
- **Missing**: No connection to company strategy; initiative exists because of historical momentum or individual advocacy

**Cross-reference:** See [frameworks-company-stages.md](frameworks-company-stages.md) for stage-appropriate strategic alignment expectations. Pre-PMF companies may have informal strategy; scale-stage companies should have explicit strategic priorities documented.

**Impact on aggregation:** When P6 is included, a "Missing" status for P6 should reduce the dimension score by 1 from the standard aggregation table. Strategic misalignment wastes even well-executed work.
